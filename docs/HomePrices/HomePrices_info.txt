date_sold: Date of sale

price: sale price (log10 units)

bedrooms: number of bedrooms

bathrooms: number of bathrooms

sqft_living: Size of living area in square feet

sqft_lot: Size of the lot in square feet

floors: number of floors

waterfront: binary indicator (‘1’ if the property has a waterfront, ‘0’ if not) for a waterfront view

view: rating of the quality of the view (An index from 0 to 4 of how good the view of the property was)

condition: condition of the house (poor to very good)

sqft_above: size (Square feet) of living space above ground

sqft_basement: size (Square feet) of living space below ground

yr_built: year build

year_renovated): year renovated and, if not renovated, the year built

zip_code: 5 digit zip code

latitude: latitude

longitude: longitude

nn_sqft_living: Average size of interior housing living space for the closest 15 houses, in square feet

nn_sqft_lot: Average size of land lots for the closest 15 houses, in square feet
